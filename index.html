ELTMaestro

Data movement is a major source of bottlenecks in analytics. Traditional ETL requires movement between three seperate servers. ELTMaestro eliminates this bottleneck by Loading and Transforming on the same machine.

At the same time, ELTMaestro is familiar and easy to learn for developers with experience in traditional ETL tools.
